# Fluffy Pancakes

## Description

Classic breakfast pancakesâ€”light and fluffy.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp melted butter

## Directions

1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
2. Make a well and whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat. Pour batter to form pancakes.
4. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden brown.
