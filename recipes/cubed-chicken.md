# Cubed Chicken

## Description

Cubed chicken to go with various dishes.
2-4 servings (depending on size), 10 min prep time, 20 min cook time.

## Ingredients

- 1 chicken breast
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp lemon pepper
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp chicken tikka masala (Shan brand preferred)

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken breast into cubes.
2. Add cubes into a pressure cooker with oil and some water.
3. Add all spices and mix well.
4. Cook in pressure cooker for 15-20 minutes, or about 4 whistles.
